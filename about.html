Who We Are

Coolwairr Was Founded
with a Single Belief

Riders should never have to choose between comfort and safety.

As temperatures rise and traffic worsens, riders need solutions built for real-world conditions — not imported assumptions. India's roads, India's heat, and India's riders are different. We built Coolwairr specifically for them.

Our Mission

To create advanced cooling solutions that make riding safer, more comfortable, and more sustainable in hot climates. Every rider — from daily commuter to weekend tourer — deserves to ride without compromise.

Our Vision

To become the global leader in rider-focused thermal management systems, starting with India. We believe the future of riding is cool — literally and figuratively.

What We're Building

Wearable cooling systems for riders. Climate-adaptive riding gear technology. Safety-first innovations for daily and adventure riders across India and beyond.

The Problem

Riders Shouldn't Have to Choose

Indian riders face extreme heat, humidity, traffic congestion, and long riding hours — every single day. Traditional riding jackets trap heat, forcing riders to choose between their safety and their comfort.

Many riders remove their safety gear entirely because of the heat. This creates a safety crisis on Indian roads that no one was addressing.

Coolwairr eliminates that compromise.
The Solution

India's First Active Cooling System

We developed a breakthrough compressor-based cooling unit paired with a specialized vest — engineered specifically for bike riders in extreme temperatures. Our solution makes long rides easier, daily commutes comfortable, and safety gear practical year-round.

  • Wearable cooling systems for all types of riders
  • Climate-adaptive riding gear technology
  • Safety-first innovations for Indian roads
