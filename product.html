CE Level 2 riding jackets

Safety gear, finally wearable in Indian summers.

For the first time, safety jackets become practical in Indian summers. Coolwairr integrates seamlessly with your existing riding gear.

  • Does not interfere with armor or impact protection zones

Component 03

Portable Cooling Unit

Engineered refrigeration adapted for the human body.

The Coolbox is a compact compressor-based cooling system that mounts directly on your motorcycle, providing continuous chilled water to the vest.

  • Compact compressor-based cooling system
  • Designed specifically for motorcycle mounting
  • DC-powered for seamless on-road use
  • Built for vibration, dust, and rough riding conditions
  • Smart temperature control display
  • Operates on battery for 3–4 hours
