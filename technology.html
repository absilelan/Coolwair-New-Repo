The Core Difference

Active vs. Passive Cooling

Coolwairr uses active thermal regulation technology — not passive airflow or evaporative cooling.
01
Active Cooling Technology

A compact refrigeration system actively removes heat from your body. Unlike mesh jackets or ventilation, the cooling effect is constant — regardless of speed or airflow.

02
Consistent at Traffic Stops

Traditional mesh jackets only cool when you're moving. Coolwairr maintains consistent cooling even at complete standstills — where Indian riders suffer most.

03
Independent of Speed

Works in city traffic, on highways, and off-road alike. The compressor drives cooling independently of bike speed or ambient airflow conditions.
